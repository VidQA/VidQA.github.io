Hochschule Hannover

Faculty III - Data|H Institute

The Hochschule Hannover (HsH) is a university of applied sciences and arts with approximately 10,000 students across five faculties. The VidQA project is co-coordinated via the institute for applied data science (Data|H) of faculty III, the faculty for media, information and design.

Data|H is a newly formed institute that aims to continue and broaden the work of the "Smart Data Analytics" research cluster. Research at this institute is driven by four main pillars: Data Mining, Data Management, Data integration and Data Analytics & Visualisation. By combining and building upon these pillars Data|H strives to develop integrated solutions to central questions regarding the topic of data science. This manifests in the development of technical solutions and applications as is also planned for the VidQA project. A majority of the research the HsH contributes to this project stems from this institute.
